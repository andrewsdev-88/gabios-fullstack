# PRD: G Gabi Produções - "The Strategic Glow" ## Vision & Concept The "Liquid Reveal" effect is a metaphor for digital transformation. The site contrasts the "Raw Process" (Camada A) with the "Strategic Glow/Authority" (Camada B). ## Visual Narrative (The Reveal) - **Layer A (The Raw):** Behind-the-scenes, production sets, raw technical lighting, "common" recording moments. - **Layer B (The Positioning):** The final result, cinema-grade color grading, high-impact typography, authority aura, rising engagement metrics. - **Liquid Effect:** Represents the "Agency Filter" that elevates the common to extraordinary. ## Innovative Features - **Liquid Metrics Scroll:** Numbers "sprout" like mercury drops during scroll. - **Transformation Grid:** Client cards with liquid hover revealing Before vs. After positioning. - **The Viral Pulse:** Background with a subtle sine wave pulse mimicking social media trend graphs. ## Design System: "High-End Social" - **Palette:** Deep Black (#0A0A0A) background, Electric Lime (#D2FF00) or Silver Chrome accents. - **Typography:** Ultra-modern Sans-Serif (Mona Sans/Inter Tight) with varied weights. Italic titles for "movement/trend" feel. - **Magnetic Buttons:** "Talk to the Strategist" CTA with magnetic cursor attraction and liquid borders. ## Key Components 1. **Hero "Positioning Reveal":** Dual-layer interaction with "Liquid Physics" (stiffness: 60, damping: 25) and subtle "Digital Glitch" edges. 2. **Social Engine:** Infinite testimonial feed with skew effect based on scroll speed. 3. **Transformation Cards:** High-standard aesthetic showcasing client authority.
